4. What happens at Prototypical?

Participants may attend workshops, join us for mini-events, and compete for prizes by creating original cardboard prototypes.

5. Are materials and supplies provided?

We will provide box cutters, x-acto knives, cutting mats, tape, and hot glue for each team. We have also partnered with the Hive and Invention Studio; participants may use any machinery or supplies at these makerspaces for their projects.

6. Will there be prizes?

Yes, there will be prizes for the top teams and raffle prizes for all participants.

7. How many people are needed to form a team?

We accept teams of up to 4 people.

8. Does it cost money to sign up?

No, this is a free event.

9. Will there be swag?

All participants will receive swag while supplies last.

10. Will there be food?

Yes, we will be serving lunch during the event
